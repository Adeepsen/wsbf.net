Booking Information

If you are looking to play in a WSBF event, email events@wsbf.net. In your email, please include the following:

  • Group/Act name
  • Genre
  • Home town
  • Website
  • Link to a live performance video
  • Any other relevant information

You should receive a follow up email letting you know that your information has been received and you have been added to our database.

NOTE: Since WSBF is a part of Clemson University, there is an extensive process that goes into booking shows. If a show is already announced, there will be no additions unless you are playing for free.
