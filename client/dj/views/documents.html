Music Policy

  • Songs must not use any of the 7 dirty words, and may not contain sexually explicit language
  • Once per hour, DJs can play two tracks from the same band, but they must be back-to-back.
  • No artist can be played on two consecutive shows.
  • Rotation Show: 75% Rotation - 25% from DJs personal collection or the WSBF Library
    • Music must be from artists that have not had a Billboard Hot 100 Hit in the last 10 years
    • Or had an a album go RIAA Gold (or greater) in the last 5 years

  • Specialty Show: No rotation music required
    • Music must be from artists that have not had a Billboard Hot 100 Hit in the last 5 years
    • Or have not had an album go RIAA Gold (or greater) in the last 2 years

Gold-Platinum Check: RIAA
